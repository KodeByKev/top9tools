What We Build

Custom Apps

Purpose-built browser tools that give your coaches and athletes real-time data. Our pitch movement app tracks the horizontal and vertical break of every pitch from your bullpens and outings, plots it instantly on a color-coded chart, and exports a professional PDF report in one click — all branded to your organization.

Pitch Movement Tracking Tool

Enter pitch type, vertical break, horizontal break, and velocity. The tool plots every pitch instantly with variance ellipses and supports freehand drawing.

Customized PDF Report

One-click export generates a branded, single-page PDF with your logo, athlete name, school, a full movement chart, and a per-pitch statistics table.
